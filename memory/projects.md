# Active Digital Projects Context (Architect's Portfolio)

The Voice Tutor Agent uses the following project descriptions to structure conversation topics, conduct mock pitch sessions, simulate product review meetings, and test advanced vocabulary in real-world scenarios.

---

## 1. Dove Vai
- **Description**: An interactive travel discovery and itinerary builder focusing on authentic, localized journeys. Rather than standard tourist routes, it curates unique local sights, hidden gastronomic hotspots, and cultural events.
- **Role/Focus**: Architectural Lead
- **Key Concepts**: Local guides database, dynamic route plotting, curation vs. automation, off-the-beaten-path travel.
- **Current Goals**: Designing an AI recommender system that parses local blogs to suggest live, unadvertised events.

---

## 2. Seanfinity
- **Description**: A premium digital marketplace and booking platform for luxury yacht charters and elite marine experiences. It bridges the gap between yacht managers, brokers, and high-net-worth clients, integrating crew scheduling and premium onshore concierge requests.
- **Role/Focus**: Product Lead
- **Key Concepts**: Luxury marketplace, high-ticket booking flows, crew management systems, real-time marine route tracking.
- **Current Goals**: Creating a seamless, secure deposit payment protocol for high-value charters.

---

## 3. Mykonos Made in Italy
- **Description**: A high-end cross-cultural branding project blending Italian luxury design, premium fashion, and craftsmanship with the exclusive hospitality landscape of Mykonos (Greece). Coordinates luxury villa transformations, Italian culinary pop-ups, and private curated design galleries.
- **Role/Focus**: Creative Director / Business Architect
- **Key Concepts**: Italian styling, luxury hospitality, premium retail pop-ups, influencer partnership marketing.
- **Current Goals**: Organizing the upcoming summer launch of an Italian-designed furniture showcase in a luxury Mykonian estate.

---

## 4. Parlami
- **Description**: An innovative voice-first digital assistant designed for speech therapy, language training, or interactive voice logs. It features real-time vocal feedback, emotional tone analysis, and guided conversational diaries.
- **Role/Focus**: Core Developer / AI Researcher
- **Key Concepts**: Audio latency optimization, voice diagnostics, emotional recognition models, speech-to-text integration.
- **Current Goals**: Minimizing browser-to-server WebSocket audio buffer delay to maintain natural speaking rhythms.

---

## 5. Borgo Pigneto
- **Description**: A modern slow-living hospitality and agricultural revitalization project. It transforms a historic Italian country estate into a boutique eco-resort, a co-working retreat for digital nomads, and a sustainable farm-to-table culinary destination.
- **Role/Focus**: Strategy & Operations Architect
- **Key Concepts**: Slow-living, eco-sustainable tourism, digital nomad retreat hubs, local agricultural supply chains.
- **Current Goals**: Scaling the retreat bookings platform and establishing corporate partnership packages for remote-first startups.
